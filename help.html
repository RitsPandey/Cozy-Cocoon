Damaged Products:

If you receive a damaged product, please contact our customer support team immediately. To initiate a return, you will need to provide us with clear photographic or video evidence of the damaged product. Our team will assess the situation and guide you through the return process.

Non-Returnable and Non-Exchangeable Products:

Due to hygiene reasons and to ensure the safety and well-being of all our customers, we cannot accept returns or exchanges for products once they have been delivered. We strictly adhere to this policy to maintain the highest standards of hygiene and product integrity.

Refunds:

If your return request for a damaged product is approved, we will initiate a refund to your original method of payment. Please note that the refund process may take a 5 to 7 business days to complete, depending on your bank. We appreciate your patience in this matter.

Incorrect or Incomplete Orders:

If you receive an incorrect or incomplete order, please contact our customer support team immediately. We will work diligently to resolve the issue and ensure you receive the correct products as soon as possible.

Cancelled Orders:

Once an order has been placed and confirmed, we do not accept cancellations. We advise you to review your order carefully before proceeding with any kind of payment.
